Pilot

Detailed Information

Description: A Pilot, or Aviator, operates aircraft in commercial, military or private sectors, most commonly to transport goods or passengers from one place to another. Being a pilot includes duties and responsibilities such as navigating aircraft, conducting inspections, and communicating with air traffic control.

Average Salary: The average salary for a pilot can range anywhere between $200,000-$300,000 per year depending on how much time you work, which airline/company, and position.

Employers:

  • Southwest (Commercial)
  • American (Commercial)
  • FedEx (Shipping)
  • Air Partner (Charter)

Related Careers:

  • Air Trafic Contoller: Direct airplanes as the take off and land.
  • Aeronautical Engineer: Build and design airplanes.
  • Flight Engineer: fix any mechanical issues on an airplane.
