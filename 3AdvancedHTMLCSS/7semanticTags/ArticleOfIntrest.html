History Of Cars

Tires

A tire is a hollowed out rubber cylinder that wraps around the cars rims. Modern tires were invented in 1845 and before cars they were often used along side the horse and carriage.

Steering Wheel

Steering wheels are used in most modern land vehicles, including all mass-production automobiles, buses, light and heavy trucks, as well as tractors and tanks. They were invented in 194 and first used to steer large ships.

Engines

An engine is a machine that converts fuel into mechanical energy. In cars, this energy drives the wheels and powers various systems within the vehicle.
