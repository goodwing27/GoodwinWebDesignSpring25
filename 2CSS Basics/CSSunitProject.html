Overview and Background

I started my YouTube channel in April of 2021, fresh out of the oandemic. I originally created it under the username "Super Random Shorts" and that name last for 3 years. Before last year the growth on my channel was minimal because I was never consistent to posting new and creative videos. last winter that all changed when I totally switched up my video style and niche for which I was posting videos. This led to rapid growth where I gained over 18,000 subscribers in just a 2 1/2 month span. I wanted to change the name to better show what I was posting that is why I switched the name to "Outer Eight". After going on a consistent posting spree I began to feel burnt out. I decided to stop posting for a little over a month, but instead focus on one long video, so that I could be more flexible with my time. After that video released in June I took the summer to prepare for making consistent full-length videos in the fall. this was when I created my predictions serious. I made 18 templates, so I could post one video for every single game of the NFL season. Later on, in November my shorts content picked up and I gained 6K more subscribers. Right now, I Have a 32 video long series going where I rank NFL teams based on who I'd want to see win the Super next year.
