Meet Our Speakers!

We’re happy to have some of the biggest names in football joining us today. They’ll share their stories and ideas about the game we all love.

Erik ten Hag

Bayer Leverkusen Manager

Erik ten Hag is a Dutch football manager currently leading Manchester United. Ten Hag is praised for his ability to build cohesive, attacking teams.

Gianni Infantino

Current Fifa President and Italian Football Administrator

Gianni Infantino is the president of FIFA, the group that runs world football. He has worked to make the World Cup bigger and help football grow in new places. He started leading FIFA in 2016.

Jürgen Klopp

Former Liverpool FC Manager

Jürgen Klopp is famous for being very energetic and passionate. He helped Liverpool win big trophies like the Champions League and Premier League.

Pep Guardiola

Manchester City FC Manager

Pep Guardiola is one of the best football coaches in the world. He has won many league titles and Champions League trophies with teams like Barcelona and Manchester City. He is known for playing with a lot of control and passing.

Carlo Ancelotti

Real Madrid FC Manager

Carlo Ancelotti is a very experienced coach who has won many big trophies. He has coached teams like AC Milan, Real Madrid, and Chelsea. People respect him for being calm and good at adjusting his game plans.

José Mourinho

Former Chelsea FC Manager

José Mourinho is a well-known football coach famous for being tough. He has won league titles in several countries and two Champions League trophies. He is also known for being very confident and outspoken.
