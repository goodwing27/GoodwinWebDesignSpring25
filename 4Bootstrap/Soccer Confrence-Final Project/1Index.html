Join us for the WCFC 2025 to discuss all things World Cup and Football!

Star Players

Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo has been one of the biggest names in football for years. He’s played for top clubs like Manchester United, Real Madrid, and Juventus, and now he plays for Al-Nassr. He’s appeared in five World Cups for Portugal and helped his country win Euro 2016.

Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi is famous for his incredible dribbling and vision. After spending most of his career in Europe, he moved to Inter Miami and continues to play at a high level. His most memorable moment came in 2022 when he led Argentina to a World Cup win, finally adding the one title that he had not not won previously.

Kylian Mbappé

Kylian Mbappé made a huge impact on the World Cup stage at just 19 years old, helping France win it all in 2018. Since then, he’s become one of the top players in the world, known for his speed and scoring. He was the top scorer in the 2022 World Cup and is expected to be a major force in international football for years to come.
